Purpose

The purpose of this website includes:

  • Sharing informations on our products and services like ongoing promotions, discount, update in prices and upgrade in services.
  • Taking sales orders

Audience

The audience for this website includes but is not limited to healthy lifestyle loving individuals and families, health enthusiaists, and medical personnel looking for for the best. This website will provide answers to their enquiries on all product and services, enable them make orders from their comfort zone and keep them informed on the latest health tips. This site will be accessed on desktops, laptops, tablets and mobile phones.
